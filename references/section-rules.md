# Section Rules

## Fixed Structure
1. `引言`
2. `2、数据信息分析`
3. `3、反馈意见分析`
4. `3.1 积极反馈`
5. `3.2 待改进反馈`
6. `4、附件-问卷题目内容`

Do not add, remove, merge, or reorder sections.

## Chapter 1 Rules
- `引言` should:
  - establish patient disease-management context
  - connect the product to patient experience or self-management
  - introduce 3-4 representative dimensions plus total dimension count when appropriate
  - close with report purpose
- Avoid doctor language such as prescribing strategy, risk stratification, or clinical decision path.

## Chapter 2 Rules
- One subsection per question.
- Subheading format: `· 维度名称`
- Title must come from the question meaning, not a direct copy of the question.
- Before writing the paragraph, classify the question into one of these patterns:
  - overall-recognition
  - conditional-recognition
  - behavioural-split
  - pain-point-attention
- Then choose matching phrasing from `expression-modules.md`.
- Each subsection must:
  1. reflect key percentages accurately
  2. explain patient experience, behaviour, convenience, or understanding
  3. connect to self-management or support logic
  4. avoid medical proof language
- Preferred length: 220-320 Chinese characters.
- Do not write low-information text like:
  - `A+B很高，C+D很低`
  - `整体较好`
  without explaining what patients are actually feeling, doing, or worrying about.

## Chapter 3 Rules
- `积极反馈` and `待改进反馈` are thematic summaries.
- Do not repeat chapter 2 question by question.
- Each item should use a title plus one paragraph body.
- Use percentage evidence from chapter 2.
- Positive feedback should focus on:
  - experience recognition
  - convenience
  - affordability or acceptable burden
  - guidance clarity
- Improvement feedback should focus on:
  - adherence
  - misunderstanding or knowledge gaps
  - price pressure
  - behaviour execution difficulty
  - support needs

## Chapter 4 Rules
- Preserve original question order.
- Preserve original option meaning.
- Only organize, do not analyze.
