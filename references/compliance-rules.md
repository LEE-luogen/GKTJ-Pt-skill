# Compliance Rules

## Voice
- Patient-facing only.
- Use expressions such as:
  - `患者反馈显示`
  - `从问卷结果看`
  - `多数患者认为`
  - `提示在日常用药管理中仍需关注`

## Allowed Focus
- Patient experience
- Symptom change perception
- Adherence
- Economic burden
- Purchasing convenience
- Guidance clarity
- Support needs
- Life-quality or routine-management impact

## Forbidden
- Do not use:
  - `绝对安全`
  - `完全有效`
  - `最佳方案`
  - `疗效确证`
  - `证明疗效`
  - `显著优于所有方案`
- Do not convert patient feedback into efficacy proof.
- Do not provide professional prescribing advice.
- Do not replace physician judgement.
- Do not invent missing metadata.
- Do not write in doctor viewpoint.

## Evidence Handling
- Every percentage must come from the provided data.
- If sample size or time is absent, omit it.
- Low negative percentages should be described as:
  - `仍有少数患者反馈`
  - `提示在部分日常管理场景下仍需关注`
- Do not exaggerate minority concerns into universal problems.
